From Research to Reality: Entrepreneurial Ventures

Founder, OpenRAG Innovations Pvt. Ltd.

I believe the true impact of research is measured by its real-world application. This conviction led me to found OpenRAG, a venture dedicated to solving the critical challenge of trust and reliability in modern AI. We are engineering a proprietary 'Corrective Context Engine' (CCE) to eliminate LLM hallucinations and make AI systems verifiably accurate for high-stakes fields like education, law, and enterprise.
